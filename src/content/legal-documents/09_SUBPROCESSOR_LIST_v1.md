## 1. Purpose

This Subprocessor List identifies material third-party providers and subprocessors used by DXBMARK to deliver services, operate its website, process payments, schedule meetings, provide support, host infrastructure, deploy software, manage CRM and project workflows, and process customer-related data.

This list supports DXBMARK's Privacy Policy, Data Processing Agreement, Cookie Policy, Security Statement, and SLA / Support Policy. Provider use may vary depending on the service, customer project, infrastructure requirements, payment method, customer location, written agreement, and technical configuration.

---

## 2. Scope

This list may apply where DXBMARK:

- Processes personal data as a processor on behalf of a B2B customer
- Uses service providers to support controller activities described in the Privacy Policy
- Provides SaaS, hosting, support, development, automation, integration, CRM, dashboard, portal, or website services
- Uses third-party platforms for payments, meetings, support, analytics, consent management, deployment, communication, or infrastructure

Not every provider listed is used for every customer or service.

## 5. Provider Changes

DXBMARK may update this Subprocessor List from time to time as services, infrastructure, providers, payment methods, tools, customer needs, legal requirements, and business operations evolve.

For active B2B customers where DXBMARK acts as processor and where required by applicable law or written agreement, DXBMARK will provide reasonable notice of material new subprocessors or material changes to subprocessors.

Customers may object to a new material subprocessor on reasonable data protection grounds within the objection period stated in the applicable agreement or notice.

Some providers may be selected, owned, controlled, or instructed by the customer. Where a customer owns or controls a provider account, the customer is responsible for:
