# Commercial Package Strategy: DXBMARK Digital Service Packages

This document defines the official commercial parameters, positioning, target customers, and operational boundaries for DXBMARK's initial Digital Service Packages.

---

## 1. Candidate Package List & Target Positioning

| Package Name | Market Positioning | Target Customer | Business Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Website Launch** | **Entry Offer** (Low barrier, speed-focused) | Solopreneurs, new startups, local businesses needing an immediate web presence. | Provide an official digital landing page or "business card" with zero development overhead. |
| **Business Presence** | **Core Offer** (Professional, multi-page) | Established SMBs, local service providers (clinics, agencies) seeking credibility. | Build a structured, multi-page informational website that establishes trust and authority. |
| **Growth Setup** | **Premium Offer** (Conversion-focused) | Rapidly growing SMBs looking to capture leads and integrate scheduling/CRMs. | Drive customer conversions and lead capture through dynamic calls to action. |
| **Commerce Starter Setup** | **Commerce Offer** (Starter e-commerce) | Small retail shops or service businesses launching their first digital store. | Establish a basic WooCommerce platform for catalog sales and payment collection. |

---

## 2. Package Scopes, Exclusions & Relationship Rules

### Website Launch
- **Scope**: 1-page professional landing page, fully mobile responsive, contact/WhatsApp integration, basic on-page SEO setup, deployment and launch.
- **Exclusions**: No custom domain/email included, no multiple pages, no database or user portal features.
- **Included Rules**: None. All domain/email setups are optional add-ons.
- **Renewal Policy**: N/A (Standard project delivery).

### Business Presence
- **Scope**: Up to 4 structured business pages (Home, About, Services, Contact), structured content hierarchy, basic analytics readiness.
- **Exclusions**: Custom domain/email are optional add-ons. Post-delivery content updates subject to separate fees.
- **Included Rules**: None.
- **Renewal Policy**: N/A (Standard project delivery).

### Growth Setup
- **Scope**: Up to 6 conversion-focused pages, lead capture integrations (WhatsApp, scheduling triggers), growth add-on compatibility.
- **Exclusions**: No custom portal software, SaaS workflows, or complex databases.
- **Included Rules**: None.
- **Renewal Policy**: N/A (Standard project delivery).

### Commerce Starter Setup
- **Scope**: WordPress + WooCommerce setup, theme-based build, up to 5 core pages, basic shop structure, up to 10 product uploads.
- **Exclusions**: Payment gateway transaction fees not included, shipping/delivery vendor fees excluded, marketplace/multi-vendor features excluded, daily catalog management excluded.
- **Included Rules**:
  - `domain-first-year` (Included, covers registration/connection for Year 1. Separate renewal required for Year 2).
  - `hosting-first-year` (Included, covers managed hosting for Year 1. Separate renewal required for Year 2).
  - `maintenance-checkups-first-year` (Included, covers 3 structured maintenance checkups during Year 1).
- **Required Setup Rules**:
  - `payment-gateway-setup` (Required setup assistance to connect payment providers like Stripe or local gateways).
- **Renewal Policy**: Annual hosting and domain renewal managed by the client or via DXBMARK's managed services in a future billing phase.

## 3. Operational Risks, Delivery Assumptions & Expectations

- **Delivery Assumptions**:
  - Clients provide all final copy, branding assets, and high-resolution images.
  - Development starts only after the design checklist and copy assets are signed off.
- **Operational Risks**:
  - Scope creep on the "WordPress + WooCommerce" package. Product variations, complex shipping rules, and custom email notifications must be gated as out-of-scope.
  - Client delays in gateway approval (Stripe, local acquirers) can stall launch. The "Payment Gateway Setup Assistance" covers configuration help but does not guarantee merchant account approval.
- **Customer Expectation Notes**:
  - Annual renewals for domain and hosting are critical. Strategy must prevent website suspensions due to unpaid renewals.
  - Marketing copy must clearly state that payment processing fees are charged directly by the gateway (e.g. Stripe transaction fees) and not by DXBMARK.

## 4. Google, Search, and Visibility Services

This section clarifies the service definitions and deliverables for DXBMARK's visibility portfolio:

- **Google Business Profile Setup**: Google Maps/Search business registry connection. Helps with local search presence, but verification approval and ranking are subject to Google's validation process. Product catalog upload is not included.
- **Google Analytics / Google Tag Setup**: Connection of Google Analytics, Google Tag, and Search Console foundations to measure site activity.
- **Basic SEO Setup**: Included as a foundation in Website Launch, Business Presence, and Growth Setup. Covers title/meta tag configurations and clean URL structures.
- **Advanced SEO Pack**: Deep technical site optimization, structured schema markup, and speed index tuning. Optional or sold as a separate scope.
- **Basic GEO Setup**: Optimizing site entity markers, keywords, and reference citations for Generative AI search systems (e.g., Perplexity, ChatGPT).
- **Basic AEO Setup**: Restructuring content into FAQs, direct-answer styles, and markup for voice/AI summary widgets.
- **Search Visibility Bundle**: Packages Advanced SEO, GEO, and AEO into a single bundle for maximum search exposure.

### Exclusion Note
- **No Ranking Guarantees**: DXBMARK provides technical alignment and optimization but does not guarantee ranking positions, page positions, or visitor volumes on search engines.

### Cloud & Infrastructure Positioning
- **Cloud & Infrastructure Setup**, including hosting architecture, DNS, CDN, Cloudflare, Vercel, deployment pipeline, or custom infrastructure, is treated as a separate service or custom proposal unless explicitly included in a package.
