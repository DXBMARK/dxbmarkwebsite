# Commercial Package Strategy: DXBMARK Digital Service Packages (v2)

This document defines the official commercial parameters, positioning, target markets, packages, and operational boundaries for DXBMARK's Digital Service Packages.

---

## 1. Market Focus & Brand Positioning

### 1.1 Market Focus
- **Geographic Targeting**: UAE (United Arab Emirates) first, followed by Egypt in a later expansion phase.
- **Language Priority**: English-first setups, with Arabic translation options scheduled for a subsequent phase.
- **Audience Context**: Targeting diverse business markets (Arabic, Western, Indian, and mixed-market clientele).
- **Delivery Limits**: DXBMARK is currently solo-operated, meaning package scopes must strictly gate and protect delivery capacity.

### 1.2 Brand Positioning & Principles
- **Positioning**: **Premium-Affordable**. DXBMARK is explicitly not positioned as the cheapest provider in the market. Entry/economic pricing applies exclusively to the "Website Launch" package.
- **Core Principle**: **Build. Run. Scale.**
- **Business Strategy**: Focus on selling business continuity (managed hosting, updates, and optimization support) rather than one-time project delivery.

### 1.3 Commercial Hierarchy
All offerings must follow this sequence:
1. Client Needs First
2. Package Value Configuration
3. Included / Required / Optional / Excluded Scopes
4. Package Name & Pricing (Prices resolved last, only after market research and owner approval)

## 2. Included Value Boosters

Every package (Website Launch, Business Presence, Growth Setup, and Commerce Starter Setup) includes the following value boosters by default:
- **Basic SEO foundation**: On-page meta tags and URL structure.
- **WhatsApp chat integration**: Direct client floating capture button.
- **Free DXBMARK temporary subdomain option**: Fast staging/hosting before custom domain connection.
- **Basic Google Analytics / Tag readiness**: Tag triggers prepared.
- **Basic launch checklist**: DXBMARK pre-flight validation.
- **Basic speed/mobile check**: Responsive performance verify.
- **Mobile/Tablet responsive design**: Visual parity on modern devices.
- **7-day post-launch correction window**: Post-launch debugging support.
- **OpenGraph / social sharing preview setup**: Rich link cards for social media.
- **Structured scope document**: Fully detailed scope boundary mapping.
- **Tawk.to chatbot widget setup**: Configured where chatbot/chat widget is included.
- **Basic indexing readiness check**: Robots.txt and sitemaps.
- **Contact/lead test before launch**: Interactive validation.

*Note: Handover documentation is excluded from standard package handouts for this phase.*

## 3. Package Architecture

### 3.1 Website Launch
- **Scope**: Entry/launch landing page or light landing structure (includes Landing, Contact, and About sections/pages). Launches on a free DXBMARK temporary subdomain. WhatsApp/direct contact button included.
- **Exclusions**: No custom domain included by default. A basic contact form is not included by default (to avoid backend, SMTP, storage, validation, and anti-spam complexity); it is available as a paid add-on.

### 3.2 Business Presence
- **Scope**: Up to 4 structured business pages (e.g. Home, About, Services, Contact) matching SMB, local service provider, or clinical needs. Page counts are flexible depending on requirements; extra pages are treated as optional add-ons.

### 3.3 Growth Setup
- **Scope**: Up to 6 conversion-centric pages. Stronger lead capture integration, Tawk.to chatbot setup, and a Logo Intro Video (HD 720p with royalty-cleared music) included.
- **Exclusions**: FHD 1080p logo video is a paid add-on. No custom portal databases or complex SaaS dashboards.

### 3.4 Commerce Starter Setup
- **Scope**: WordPress + WooCommerce basic store setup. Up to 5 pages and 10 catalog products. Includes 3 monthly checkups during the first 3 months. Google Business Profile setup is included (if the client does not already have one).
- **Required Setup**: Payment Gateway Setup Assistance is mandatory.
- **Exclusions**: Custom domain/hosting are included only if the approved package price explicitly covers them. Gateway, payment provider, shipping provider, and merchant account fees are paid directly to the provider.

### 3.5 Future & Custom Offerings (Out-of-Checkout Scope)
- **Commerce Advanced / Commerce Expert**: Larger catalogs, dedicated hosting, advanced WooCommerce, or performance-sensitive systems. Custom proposal only.
- **Custom eCommerce Platform**: Fully custom web app storefront, custom database, admin console, and dedicated cloud nodes. Custom proposal only.
- **Shopify Store Setup**: Proposal-based Shopify catalog setup, theme connection, and apps matching client requests.

## 4. Specific Service Policies

### 4.1 Logo Intro Video Policy
- ** growth & Commerce**: HD 720p video included. Social media and website licensing royalty-cleared.
- **Paid Upgrades**: FHD 1080p resolution is available as a paid add-on.
- **Website Launch & Business Presence**: Available as a paid add-on.

### 4.2 Progressive Web App (PWA) Conversion
- Available as a paid add-on across all packages to convert the site into an installable mobile/desktop Progressive Web App.
- **Exclusion**: Submission/publishing to Apple App Store or Google Play Store is excluded.

### 4.3 Domain & Hosting Policy
- Custom domains can be purchased/connected as an add-on across all packages.
- Staging/fast launch on a free DXBMARK subdomain is optional.
- High-performance hosting must be prioritized for e-commerce. Weak shared hosting is excluded for serious business sites.
- Client-owned servers remain client responsibility unless a managed support plan is active.
- First suitable WordPress hosting reference is approximately $145/year including eligible domain options (not final public pricing).

### 4.4 Support, Maintenance, & Corrections
- **7-day correction window**: Stated bugs and content typos corrected at no additional charge.
- **Website Maintenance Checkup (Add-on)**: A one-time checkup lasting up to 7 days, including core updates review, security review, file/code cleanup, and PageSpeed review.
- **Commerce Starter Setup**: 3 monthly checkups during the first 3 months included.

### 4.5 Legal Pages Packages
- **Basic Legal Pages Pack**: Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy.
- **Advanced Legal Pages Pack**: Basic Pack plus: Refund & Cancellation Policy, Delivery & Fulfillment, Security Statement, SLA & Support, Acceptable Use, DPA, and Subprocessors.
- *Compliance Note: Legal pages are drafts for compliance review only. DXBMARK does not provide legal advice; final human legal review is a client responsibility.*

## 5. Pricing & Proposal Workflow

### 5.1 Pricing Principles
- All official base rates and resolved Stripe catalog amounts are USD-based.
- No client-side currency calculations or exchange rate factors.
- Website Launch may support fixed direct checkout. Other packages will use proposal-first configuration with starting-from placeholders.
- **Pricing Research Gate**: Pricing research is handled in Phase 2M. Final prices are not approved until the owner signs off on [pricing-research.md](file:///Users/sunmarke/Downloads/untitled%20folder%202/dxbmark-website/specs/002-digital-service-packages/pricing-research.md).

### 5.2 Proposal vs Checkout Workflow
- **Website Launch**: Direct self-service checkout.
- **Business Presence, Growth Setup, Commerce Starter**: Configurations initiate a consultation request.
- **Zoho Integration**: Approved custom scopes are converted into custom Zoho invoices or Stripe Checkout payment links.
- **Initial consultations**: Up to 30 minutes pre-purchase or up to 60 minutes post-purchase.
